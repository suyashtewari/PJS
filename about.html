About our group

The PJs crew is comprised of a group of high school programmers that also know to have fun. What started as a small summer break project turned full fledged game with new mechanics, PJs aims to combine both casual gameplay and strategic maneuvering through the website game we call PJs, or Pathetic Jumping Squirrels.

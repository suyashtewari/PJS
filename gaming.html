Your Identity:

You are guessing -person-'s identity.

Instructions:

  • To start off, a randomly selected person asks a YES/NO question to another member in the party about his/her identity.
  • The member asked answers the question and proceeds to ask a question themselves to any member in the party. This chain of questioning sets the stage for the game.
  • Whenever any player wants, they can guess another player's identity in the PJs interface using clues from the Y/N questions.
  • A pop-up will show saying if you guessed correctly or not. If you guessed someone's identity correctly, they will be eliminated from the game.
  • No one else will know the results of your guess (or the fact that someone guessed in the first place).
  • All players can ask questions throughout the entirety of the game (even if their identity has already been guessed).
  • The objective of the game is to be the last member of the party whose identity has not been discovered.
  • Tip: keep track of who you think has been eliminated and who you think still needs to be discovered!
